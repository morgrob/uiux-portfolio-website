App Team Carolina Website

View Live Site Visit Repository

This is an informational website made with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for and about App Team Carolina, a nonprofit organization that teaches UNC Computer Science student iOS development and design skills and builds apps.

The purpose of this website is to house information about the structure and purpose of App Team Carolina as well as act as a hub for applying to the organization. In practice, this website has been a very powerful marketing tool for the organization as it has brought a new level of attention to the organization.
