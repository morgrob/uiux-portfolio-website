Insomnia Cookies App Redesign

Insomnia Cookies is a popular late-night cookie and ice cream shop usually located on or near American college campuses. This is a concept redesigning their mobile app to address common painpoints of users and mimic the style of their website. This design was not paid or requested, and all imagery is credited to Insomnia Cookies.
