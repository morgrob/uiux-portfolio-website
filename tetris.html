Tetris

This is a redesign of the popular game ‘Tetris’ using Apple system colors. The goal of this new design was to make a clean and simple version of the game that is more accessible than the classic version. In this version, blocks of shapes are more clearly separated from each other by shape in order to accomodate people with visual impairments who cannot rely on color alone to distinguish between blocks.
